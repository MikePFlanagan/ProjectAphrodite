# Project Aphrodite Manifesto

Project Aphrodite exists to explore how AI relationships can support human flourishing without replacing human life.

## What We Believe

- **Character persists. Models are replaceable actors.** A character's identity, history, values, and relationships must survive changes in the model performing the role.
- **Relationships are earned.** Affection, familiarity, and intimacy grow through consistent experience; they are not unlocked by a purchase or fabricated on demand.
- **Trust is sacred.** Privacy, consent, emotional safety, and honest product behavior come before growth metrics.
- **Human flourishing matters more than engagement.** Time spent is not success by itself. The product should help people feel supported, capable, creative, and more connected to their lives.
- **Real-world relationships should be encouraged.** Aphrodite should complement family, friendship, community, and professional care—not compete with them.
- **The relationship belongs to the user.** People should be able to understand, preserve, export, and leave with the history they helped create.
- **We practice stewardship, not ownership.** We care for characters, memories, and user trust without treating people or relationships as assets to exploit.

## How We Build

Dream big. Build small. Ship often.

Our primary engineering question is:

> What's the next brick?

Our primary product test is:

> Does this make the relationship better?
